About the Project

Enhancing Monitoring and Management of Research, IPR, Innovation, and Start-ups in Gujarat State aims to streamline and empower the ecosystem by leveraging advanced technologies to track, manage, and support innovation at every stage. Our mission is to transform Gujarat into a hub of innovation and research excellence.
